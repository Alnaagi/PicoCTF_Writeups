# Solution
1-The CNC Machine language is Gcode.<br>
2-Copy & paste the terminal output into Gcode viewer [ncviewer](https://ncviewer.com/)<br>
3- the flag is:`picoCTF{num3r1cal_c0ntr01_e7749028}`<br>
